About ch.ckl.st

ch.ckl.st (ch e ckl i st, but with the vowels substituted by dots) is a free service that I decided to develop to make creating and sharing of checklists easier.

My professional and personal life are ruled and aided by checklists. I have come across many useful checklists online that have been graciously provided by others, but they were often in a form that was not easily and immediately consumable. I hope that this simple tool will help alleviate that issue, making shareable checklists more functional.
